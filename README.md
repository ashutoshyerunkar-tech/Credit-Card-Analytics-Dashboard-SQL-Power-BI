# 📊 Credit Card Weekly Analytics Dashboard (SQL + Power BI)

## 🚀 Project Overview
An end-to-end SQL + Power BI project analyzing weekly credit card transactions and customer behavior. Built relational tables in SQL, cleaned and modeled data, and developed DAX measures to track revenue trends, customer segmentation, and Week-over-Week (WoW) performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor weekly revenue and transaction metrics  
- Analyze customer demographics and income segments  
- Track WoW revenue growth  
- Identify top revenue-driving customer and expense categories  
- Build stakeholder-ready interactive dashboards  

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **SQL Server** – Data storage, cleaning & modeling  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization  
- **DAX** – KPI calculations & segmentation logic  
- **CSV datasets** – Practice data source  

---

## 🗄 Database Structure

### 🔹 cc_detail (Transaction Data)
Revenue, Interest, Transaction Amount & Count, Expense Type, Credit Limit, Week & Quarter data.

### 🔹 cust_detail (Customer Data)
Age, Gender, Education, Income, Job Category, Marital Status, Satisfaction Score.

---

## 📈 Key DAX Measures
- Age Group Segmentation (20–30, 30–40, etc.)
- Income Group (Low, Mid, High)
- Current Week Revenue
- Previous Week Revenue
- WoW Revenue %

---

## 📊 Dashboards

### 1️⃣ Credit Card Transaction Report
- Revenue & Interest KPIs  
- Revenue by Education & Expense Type  
- Quarterly Trends  
- Revenue by Card Category  

### 2️⃣ Credit Card Customer Report
- Revenue by Gender (Weekly Trend)  
- Revenue by Income & Age Group  
- Customer Acquisition Cost  
- Revenue by Job & Top States  

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- High-income customers drive the majority of revenue  
- Swipe transactions contribute the highest revenue share  
- 40–50 age group is the top-performing segment  
- Bills and Entertainment dominate spending categories
